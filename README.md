# 🌱 Habitué – Introduction  

In our day-to-day lives, we often struggle with **inconsistency**—whether it’s sticking to a routine, building a new skill, or simply maintaining healthy habits. Over time, these small breaks lead to bigger gaps in our growth.  

That’s where **Habitué** comes in.  
A personalized habit companion, it helps you stay on track by keeping a record of your daily routines, sending gentle reminders, and showing your progress through interactive visuals.  

With features like **short-term habit tracking, journaling, to-do lists, and long-term goal monitoring**, it transforms discipline into an achievable, motivating, and rewarding journey.  

The **Habit Tracker app** is designed to help users build consistency, stay motivated, and achieve their personal goals.  
✅ Real-time cloud sync  
✅ Personalized reminders  
✅ Interactive progress visualizations  
✅ Authentication for safe and secure login (Email & Google Sign-In)  

---

## 🏠 Home Page  
The home screen of **Habitué** welcomes users with:  
- A personalized greeting and motivational quote  
- A visual guide of habit formation journey  
- Three interactive cards for quick access:  
  - **Habit Tracking** – log daily routines  
  - **Progress Visualization** – monitor consistency  
  - **Personal Blogging** – capture reflections & experiences  

The soothing purple theme and intuitive layout make navigation simple, engaging, and motivating.  

---

## 📆 Daily Progress Tracking  
- Track everyday habits easily.  
- Mark habits as **completed / not completed**.  
- Built-in **timer**: do your tasks with focus, and once the timer finishes, the habit is auto-marked ✅ completed.  

---

## ⏳ Short Term Progress Tracking  
- Set **small, time-bound goals** (like two-week challenges).  
- Add task details, deadlines, milestones, or rewards.  
- Track progress via:  
  - **Manual logs** for check-ins  
  - **Checklists/Subtasks** to break bigger goals  

👉 This helps visualize progress, stay motivated with rewards, and build discipline step by step.  

---

## 🏆 Long Term Progress Tracking  
- Focus on **larger goals** spanning weeks or months.  
- Create long-term goals with:  
  - Title, category, start & end dates, motivational notes  
  - Categories like **Personal, Work, Fitness** for organization  
- Regular progress updates keep users committed to **life-changing habits**.  

---

## 📊 Progress Tracking  
The **Progress Tracking screen** provides a **calendarized view** of habits.  
Red = not completed ❌  
Green = completed ✅  

---

## ✍️ Personal Blogging  
A personal space where users can:  
- **Add Notes / Journals** alongside habits  
- **Track Mistakes & Learnings** for self-reflection  
- Keep thoughts, setbacks, and lessons in one place  

This adds a deeper, more personal dimension to habit building.
